Directed by Ryan Coogler

2025  ·  R  ·  138 min  ·  Southern Gothic · Horror · Musical · Period Drama

Synopsis

Mississippi Delta, 1932. Twin brothers Smoke and Stack — veterans turned bootleggers — return to their hometown hoping to leave their troubled pasts behind. They open a juke joint, a haven for blues music and Black community. But when a coven of vampires, drawn by the otherworldly talent of a young blues prodigy named Sammie, descends on their celebration, the brothers face an evil that threatens not just their lives — but the very soul of a generation.
