Michael B. Jordan

Actor · Producer · Director

b. Feb 9, 1987  ·  Newark, NJ  ·  Academy Award Winner

Biography

Michael Bakari Jordan was born in Santa Ana, California, and raised in Newark, New Jersey. He began his acting career as a child, making his television debut before landing a breakthrough role as Wallace in HBO's The Wire (2002). His performance as Oscar Grant in Fruitvale Station (2013) established him as a dramatic force, earning widespread critical acclaim. He rose to global fame as Erik Killmonger in Black Panther (2018) and solidified his leading-man status with the Creed trilogy. In 2025, his dual performance in Ryan Coogler's Sinners earned him the Academy Award for Best Actor.
